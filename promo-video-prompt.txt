=== STRATEGIC DEPTH TO CONVEY ===

STRATEGY 1: "THE EARLY BIRD"
- Place predictions 5+ days before the session to lock in the 5.0x multiplier.
- Even a partial match (driver in Top 5 but wrong slot) at 5.0x is worth more than an exact match at 1.0x for lower positions.
- Example: P5 exact (10 pts) × 5.0x = 50 pts vs. P1 exact (25 pts) × 1.0x = 25 pts.

STRATEGY 2: "THE HEDGER"
- Use Team bets for chaotic/unpredictable sessions (wet weather, street circuits).
- You get 50% points but DOUBLE the chance of hitting since either team driver counts.
- Pair with a risky Driver bet via Combo for maximum coverage.

STRATEGY 3: "THE SPREADER"
- Don't blow all Fun-Coins on one event. With 4 bet slots per event and multiple sessions per weekend (Qualifying, Sprint Qualifying, Sprint Race, Main Race), spread predictions to accumulate more total points.
- 50 coins/weekend × strategic distribution = optimal point harvesting.

STRATEGY 4: "THE JACKPOT HUNTER"
- Go for the exact P1-P5 driver order on the Main Race for the Jackpot.
- If nobody wins, the pool rolls over — meaning later rounds have MASSIVE jackpots.
- Only driver bets qualify (not team bets), so you MUST use a driver prediction.

STRATEGY 5: "THE COMBO MAXIMIZER"
- Submit a Combo bet (Driver + Team) on the same event. Costs 2× the entry fee but earns points from BOTH slips.
- Use your Driver slip for the risky exact order prediction (Jackpot eligible).
- Use your Team slip as a safety net to guarantee some points.

STRATEGY 6: "THE MULTIPLIER + BET LIMIT OPTIMIZER"
- With only 4 bets allowed per event, place your confident predictions early (5.0x multiplier), then use remaining slots closer to race time when more information is available (practice results, weather updates) at a lower multiplier.
- Balance between conviction (high multiplier) and information (low multiplier, better predictions).
